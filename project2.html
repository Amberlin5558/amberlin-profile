Project Objective

This project involved a deep-dive analysis into Tesla's key business risks across market, technology, and reputation domains. My primary responsibility was to conduct a thorough Cost & Benefit Analysis for a series of proposed risk mitigation strategies. The goal was to provide a data-driven framework to help stakeholders make informed, financially sound decisions that balance short-term costs with long-term gains.

Cost & Benefit Analysis Showcase

My analysis broke down each proposed strategy into estimated costs and potential revenues, providing a clear financial forecast for both short-term and long-term horizons.

1. Market Risk Mitigation Strategies

Analysis of strategies to counter market competition, such as launching an entry-level EV and expanding leasing programs.

Short-Term Strategy: Launching an entry-level EV to compete with models like BYD's Seagull.

Long-Term Strategy: Expanding EV leasing and tapping into B2B fleet markets.

2. Technology Risk Mitigation Strategies

Evaluation of investments in R&D and autonomous driving features to maintain a competitive technological edge.

Short-Term Strategy: Phased rollout of autonomous driving features and building trust through data transparency.

Long-Term Strategy: Investing in fully certified L4/L5 autonomy and monetizing via software subscriptions.

3. Reputation Risk Mitigation Strategies

Analysis of actions to strengthen brand trust and manage public relations crises effectively.

Short-Term Strategy: Forming a public relations team and strengthening official speech systems.

Long-Term Strategy: Further depersonalizing the brand and enhancing corporate governance structures.

Summary Analysis & Final Recommendation

The final stage of my analysis consolidated all findings into a comprehensive summary, comparing the total costs and benefits to calculate a consolidated ROI. This provided a clear, quantifiable basis for strategic decision-making.

Analysis of an alternative "Plan B" involving aggressive market and product expansion.

Final Result: Through a combination of strategies, a high consolidated ROI of 281% was projected, demonstrating a clear path to mitigating risks while driving significant value.
